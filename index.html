Soulshine is a name I made to remind myself to stay true to my own self, and to contribute myself to that which serves others.

I am working on:

  • An IRC successor; think WASTE but it won't be broken, defunct and unscalable. More information will come as I make more progress with a proof-of-concept and alpha implementation.
  • A modular roguelike mmo server/client hybrid that places total emphasis on strategy and fairness. Think SCII meets Dwarf Fortress, meets Final Fantasy, meets EVE Online, meets Minecraft. It'll be intense. If all goes well, I'd like this to run on the PS4, but we'll see how that goes.

I write Picolisp, although I have plans to eventually get a crew together, fork Picolisp and begin morphing it into something more capable and generic.
