soulshine.github.io

Soulshine is a name I made to remind myself to stay true to my own self, and to contribute myself to that which serves others.

I am working on:

  • An IRC successor; think WASTE but it won't be broken, defunct and unscalable. More information will come as I make more progress with a proof-of-concept and alpha implementation.
  • A crawler/search engine built in Racket. Focus is on simplicity and fault-tolerance. Will probably be built around Riak, but I haven't really decided yet. (ROYAL COYOTE)
  • Lattice-based cryptography. Quantum computers threaten everything crypto has ever accomplished. It is absolutely essential that a complete crypto system be put in place. I have a few ideas for strong passphrase generation, but it won't matter if every cipher is broken from the beginning. It's not a problem of weak algorithms, but strong computers. I'm going to make the computers work against themselves.
